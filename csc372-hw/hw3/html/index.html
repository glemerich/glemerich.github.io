Your UNCG Off-Campus Food Guide

Introduction

Are you a culinary adventurer in search of the perfect meal on campus? NO!? Well, are you a UNCG student looking for something other than the on-campus dining options that won't break the bank? If so, have we got the places for you. From Latin/Asian fusion, to a NY Style deli, and a breakfast restraunt with the most delicious mexican lunch in Greensboro. All three within walking distance of campus featuring some of the most delicious food you can imagine, all for a reasonable price.

Our Top Recommendations

Bandito Bodega

1609 W Friendly Ave, Greensboro, NC 27403-1208

A unique Latin/Asian fusion restaurant located only .5 miles from UNCG, known for their "Big Ass Burrito" as well as dishes such as Quesadillas, Tacos, and Specialty Rice.

Open 7 Days A Week 11:30AM - 9:30PM

Guacamole & Chips

$10 - 8oz portion of delicous homemade guacamole and a bag of homemade tortilla chips. This snack will easily feed everyone in your dorm.

Big Ass Burrito Bowl

$14.50 - [Choose: Chicken / Pork / Chorizo / Tofu / Steak (+$2) / Shrimp (+$2) / Tuna (+$4)] plus Spanish Rice, Black Beans, Pico De Gallo, Black Bean & Corn Salsa, Radish, Red Cabbage, Cilantro, Chipotle Crema, Tomatillo Verde. Customizable to your liking, this is a delicious way to feel like you're still eating something healthy!

Southwest Pimento Burrito

$12.50 - [Choose: Chicken / Pork / Chorizo / Tofu / Steak (+$2) / Shrimp (+$2) / Tuna (+$4)] Spanish Rice, Black Beans, House Spicy Pimento, Fresh Guacamole, Salsa Verde, Black Bean & Corn, Red Cabbage, Cilantro, Jalapeno, Chipotle Crema. This burrito is the perfect combination of Tex-Mex and southern pimento cheese, not to mention it's enough food for two meals!

First Carolina Deli

1635 Spring Garden St Greensboro, NC 27403

An authentic NY Style deli located less than a mile from UNCG, stop in for a delicious sandwich, soup, burger, or salad.

Open Monday-Saturday 10AM - 9PM

Arlington

$11.25 - Fresh, shaven Boar's Head turkey topped with melty Muenster cheese, topped with crisp lettuce, sliced tomato & creamy mayonnaise, on an onion roll. This is a can't miss sandwich!

Rachel

$11.25 - Pastrami, Sauerkraut, Swiss, hot on rye with Russian dressing. A new take on an old classic, done to perfection.

Italian Sub

$11.25 - Genoa salami, cappicola, imported ham, provolone, sliced cherry peppers, lettuce, tomato, onion, special dressing on a sub roll. Who doesn't like an italian sub? This is certainly my favorite.

Bagner and Alexa's Breakfast & Lunch

1618 Spring Garden St Greensboro, NC 27403

Located just off UNCG's campus, this restraunt boasts an impressive menu featuring everything from classic breakfast meals to delicious Mexican dishes.

Open 7 Days A Week 7AM - 3PM

Spartan Special

$5.75 - 2 eggs, 1 side, choice of bacon, sausage link, or sausage patty. Served w/ toast. An affordable and delicious breakfast, even better with some homemade hot sauce.

Huevos con Chorizo

$8.50 - Chorizo, scrambled eggs. Served w/ rice, beans, and corn tortillas. A delicious take on scrambled eggs that will leave you wanting more.

Torta Cubana

$12.95 - Torta bread, stuffed with beans, carne asada, mozzarella, chorizo, salchica, ham, 1 egg, provolone cheese, crema, avocado, jalapeno, onions, tomatoes, and lettuce. A sandwich that rivals that of the deli above, with a mexican twist! A delcious, and filling, lunch.
